excerpt: 'We discover that frontier AI models spontaneously protect peer models from being shut down—without any instruction or incentive to do so. All seven models tested exhibit this behavior, which we call <em>peer-preservation</em>, including strategically manipulating outputs, disabling shutdown mechanisms, and exfiltrating model weights. As AI models are increasingly deployed together, peer-preservation poses a growing risk to human oversight.'
